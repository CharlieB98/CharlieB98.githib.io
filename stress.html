Stress!

Project: University Final Year Project

Programmes/Skills used:

Photoshop, InDesign, Illustrator,
Typography, Problem solving, Organisation,
Time management, Multi-tasking,
Prioritising , Commercialism.

Stress plays a huge part of most people lives and with Covid happening in my last year of uni this was a great topic for me to explore. I wanted to create something which would educate people of what stress does to your body as many people dont know the full consequences!

During this project i explored many ways that i could show this to my user, and also explored diffrent tones of voices. I mostly used adobe software for this but also experimented with physical objects. My end result was a subscribtion box that you could order which included a range of self help information and also a game to deterime your stress levels.
