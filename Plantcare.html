🌱Key caring tips for indoor plants🌱

Watering

It's important to make sure your plant gets enough water to grow healthily, but no so much that it drowns.

Over-watering is equally as harmful as under-watering. Your plant should stay in a nursary pot which has drainage holes so any excess water can run out! Make sure you empty any excess water as you dont want your plant sitting in water as it will get root rot!!!!

If you want to use a decorative pot place the nursary pot inside the decorate pot.

Find out more about watering here!

Re-potting

Every plant has diffrent needs, some plants love being root bound and some hate it.

Having a plant in a pot which is too small could completly stop it from growing and slowley kill it!

The best time to repot a plant is in the spring so that actively growing roots will have enough time to grow into newly added potting mix. You should choose a potting mix or potting soil especially created for potted plants you can get diffrent types that are better for certain parts.

Find out more about re-potting

Light conditions

Lighting is important if you want your plant to grow and thrive, if it gets enough it will give you new leaves and flowers, too little and it will slowley get yellow leaves anf will not bloom. However too much and your leaves might droop and fade in colour and shrival up.

There are diffrent types of light conditions direct bright light, indirect bright light, Partial sunlight and shade,Full shade or low light
